Greek Salad Dressing

American-Style Creamy Greek Dressing

This is a dressing I made for an American-style Greek salad, one with a hearty lettuce not typically found in a traditional Greek salad. This one will surely become part of your weekly rotation!

Ingredients

  • 1/2 Cucumber, chopped
  • 1/2 package brine-packed Feta
  • 1/4 cup greek Yogurt
  • 3 Tbsp milk
  • 3 cloves garlic, finely minced
  • 1/4 cup EVOO
  • 1 tsp dried oregano
  • salt to taste

Steps

  1. Process cucumber, garlic, milk, yogurt, and EVOO
  2. Add in dry spices and salt and pulse
  3. Cover and chill for 30 minutes
  4. Enjoy
